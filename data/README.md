# Dataset

- Synthetic [41]: It is a synthetic dataset, generated by the MTS generator TSAGen [42].
- MoCap [28]: Derived from the CMU motion capture repository. In this dataset, every motion is represented as a sequence of hundreds of frames.
- ActRecTut [7]: This dataset involves two participants performing hand movements with height gestures in daily life and 3D gestures while playing tennis.
- PAMAP2 [31]: Covering both basic (e.g., walking, sitting) and composite human activities (e.g., soccer), this dataset features data from eight individuals.
- UscHad [48]: This dataset encompasses 12 distinct human activities such as jumping and running, recorded for 14 individuals.
- UcrSeg [18]: This dataset encompasses diverse sources, including medical fields, insect studies, robotics, and power demand data.
